# Journey to Heaven (大闹天宫)

A mini-game inspired by the classic Chinese novel "Journey to the West" (西游记).

## 🎮 Game Overview

In this game, Sun Wukong (孙悟空) must navigate through the Heavenly Palace (天宫) while avoiding attacks from Heavenly Generals.

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Click **"Start Game"** to begin
2. Sun Wukong automatically moves along the path toward the Heavenly Palace
3. **Click on Li Tianwang (托塔李天王)** to launch a tower attack
4. **Click on Nezha (哪吒)** to send him flying toward you
5. Avoid all attacks to reach the palace and win!

## 🎯 Controls

| Character | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Li Tianwang | Click to shoot tower |
| Nezha | Click to fly attack |

## 🏆 Win Condition

Reach the Heavenly Palace without getting hit.

## 🔄 Restart

Click **"Restart"** button after winning or losing to play again.
