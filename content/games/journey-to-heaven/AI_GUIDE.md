# Journey to Heaven

## Overview

Control Sun Wukong to traverse the Heavenly Palace, avoiding attacks from enemies, to reach the palace and win.

### Core Mechanics

1. Click "Start Game" to begin
2. Sun Wukong automatically moves toward the palace
3. Click enemies to avoid their attacks
4. Dodge all attacks and reach the palace to win

### Characters

| Character | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| General | Click to launch tower attack |
| Nezha | Click to initiate flying attack |
