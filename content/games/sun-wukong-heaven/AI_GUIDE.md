# Sun Wukong at Heavenly Gate

## Overview

Sun Wukong flies freely at the Heavenly Gate. Click to interact.

### Core Mechanics

1. Sun Wukong automatically flies at the Heavenly Gate
2. Click background music button for background music
3. Click Sun Wukong to trigger:
   - Sound effect
   - Hurt expression animation
   - Mouse changes to golden ring (3 seconds)
   - Sun Wukong jumps to new position
   - Magic aura appears

### Visual Elements

- Free-flying animation
- Scene elements: Heavenly Gate, lanterns, clouds, stars, cranes
- Dynamic effects: twinkling stars, drifting clouds, fireflies, dancing cranes
